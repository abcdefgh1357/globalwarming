GLOBAL WARMING

Activity 3.1
Date - 7th July, 2022


Global warming is the observed century scale rise in the average temperature of the Earth's climate system.

Since the early 20th century, the global air and sea surface temprature has increased.

A green house gas, like carbon-dioxide, it represents around 80 percent of total greenhouse gas mass in the atmosphere and 90 percent of greenhouse gas volume. Water Vapour and clouds account for 66 to 85 percent of the greenhouse effect, compared to a range of 9 to 26 percent for CO2.
